About HiTec University Library

Your gateway to knowledge, research, and academic excellence

Our Mission

HiTec University Library is committed to providing comprehensive information resources and services to support the academic and research needs of our students, faculty, and staff. We strive to foster a culture of learning and intellectual growth.

Our Services

  • Book Lending: Borrow books for up to 15 days with automatic fine calculation for late returns
  • Digital Catalog: Search and browse our complete collection online
  • Library Card System: Unique identification for each student with card status management
  • Book Reviews: Share your thoughts and read reviews from other students
  • Reference Services: Professional assistance in finding resources

Loan Policy

Loan Duration: 15 days from the date of issue
Late Fee: PKR 50 per day for overdue books
Renewal: Books can be renewed if not requested by another student
Damage/Loss: Students are responsible for books borrowed under their card

How to Reach Us

By Car

HiTec University is located in Taxila, easily accessible from Islamabad and Rawalpindi via the Grand Trunk Road. Ample parking available on campus.

By Public Transport

Regular buses and vans are available from Islamabad and Rawalpindi to HiTec University campus. The library is centrally located within the campus.
